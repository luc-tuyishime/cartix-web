Community

Cartix is here to stay and to grow mainly through a community of open data enthusiasts driven by the spirit of
collective effort and knowledge sharing. Our main goal is to sustainably spark a movement to grow a vibrant
community of expert in data science that spans various sectors in Rwanda and beyond.

Customers

Data is continually becoming an indispensable commodity in all sectors (public, private & civil society). Cartix is built in way to cater for all the users found in those three categories. Our key added value is tailor-made dashboard. We have dashboards tailored for NGOs, MFIs, Printing Companies, Public Institutions, etc. For any dashboard customization, it is important that it be scoped in a manner to allow for high quality, well designed and properly tested application. At the beginning of every project, along with the ToRs, we first sit down with the party and figure out who will be the users of the product and how we can satisfy them. From this brainstorming we then can start driving towards a design that maximizes intended values.

Blog

Welcome to Cartix blogs, a place where everyone has a story to share. Stories shared on our blog include opinions, development, strides and challenges in the area of data science in Rwanda, the region and Africa at large. We are aware that data has no boundaries but on our platform the focus is Africa and we make sure all contributors customize their content to the African context while we think global and act local. It is your regular news on data science in Africa reimagined, straight from the people who are making and living it. Follow us as we help enrich your knowledge on topics related to data science in Rwanda, the EAC and Africa. Are you a data enthusiast and willing to contribute to our blog? Don’t be shy, drop us a line.
